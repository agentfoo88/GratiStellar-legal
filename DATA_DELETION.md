# Data Deletion Instructions for GratiStellar

This document provides clear instructions for users who want to delete their data from GratiStellar, in compliance with privacy regulations including GDPR, CCPA, and Google Play Store requirements.

---

## Quick Summary

**To delete all your data from GratiStellar:**
1. Open the app → Go to Account Settings
2. Tap "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion
4. All data will be permanently removed within 30 days

**OR** Email us at: gratistellar@gmail.com

---

## What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

### Immediately Deleted:
- Access to your account
- Authentication credentials
- Active session tokens

### Deleted Within 30 Days:
- All gratitude entries (including deleted items in Trash)
- Galaxy/collection names and metadata
- Display name
- Email address (if provided)
- App preferences and settings
- Custom colors and configurations
- All synchronized cloud data (Firebase Firestore)

### Retained for Technical Reasons:
- **Anonymized Analytics:** Aggregate, non-identifiable usage statistics (cannot be linked to you)
- **Crash Reports:** Anonymized crash logs (no personal identifiers)
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups may persist for up to 90 days, then permanently deleted

**Important:** After 30 days, data deletion is irreversible and cannot be recovered.

## Method 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

1. **Open GratiStellar**
   - Launch the app on your device

2. **Navigate to Settings**
   - Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left corner
   - Select "Account" from the menu

3. **Access Account Settings**
   - Scroll down to the "Account Management" section

4. **Delete Account**
   - Tap "Delete Account" button
   - Read the confirmation dialog carefully
   - Confirm that you understand data cannot be recovered

5. **Final Confirmation**
   - Enter your display name or type "DELETE" to confirm
   - Tap "Permanently Delete"

6. **Confirmation**
   - You'll receive a confirmation message
   - You'll be signed out immediately
   - Data deletion begins automatically

### What Happens Next:

- **Immediate:** You lose access to your account
- **Within 24 hours:** All active data is marked for deletion
- **Within 30 days:** All data is permanently erased from our servers
- **No recovery:** Data cannot be restored after deletion begins

## Method 3: Delete Only Specific Data

If you want to delete specific gratitude entries but keep your account:

### Delete Individual Entries:

1. Open the entry you want to delete
2. Tap the trash icon
3. The entry moves to Trash (recoverable for 30 days)

### Permanently Delete from Trash:

1. Tap menu (☰) → "Trash"
2. Long-press the entry you want to permanently delete
3. Tap "Delete Forever"
4. Confirm deletion

### Empty All Trash:

1. Go to Trash
2. Tap "Empty Trash" button
3. Confirm to permanently delete all items

## Before You Delete: Export Your Data

We recommend exporting your data before deletion:

### How to Export:

1. Open GratiStellar
2. Tap menu (☰) → "Export Backup"
3. Follow the prompts to create an encrypted backup file
4. Save the file to your device or cloud storage (Google Drive, etc.)
5. **Keep this file safe** - it cannot be recovered after account deletion

### What's Included in Export:
- All gratitude entries
- Galaxy/collection names
- App preferences
- Timestamps and metadata

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I recover my data after deletion?
**A:** No. Once deletion is confirmed, it cannot be undone. Export your data first if you might want it later.

### Q: How long does deletion take?
**A:** Immediate access removal, full deletion within 30 days.

### Q: What if I change my mind?
**A:** If you email us within 24 hours of deletion request, we may be able to stop the process. No guarantees after 24 hours.

### Q: Will deleting the app delete my data?
**A:** No! Uninstalling the app does NOT delete your cloud data. You must follow the account deletion process.

### Q: Can I delete only cloud data and keep local data?
**A:** Not currently. Deletion removes both local and cloud data. Export first if you want to keep a copy.

### Q: What about backups I've made?
**A:** Personal backup files you've exported are yours to keep or delete. We don't have access to exported backup files.

### Q: Does anonymizing my account delete data?
**A:** No. Only full account deletion removes data. Anonymous accounts still store your gratitude entries.
